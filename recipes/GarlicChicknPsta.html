Creamy Garlic Chicken Pasta

Description

This creamy garlic chicken pasta is indulgent yet simple - tender chicken pieces coated in a velvety garlic and parmesan sauce, tossed through perfectly cooked pasta. It's an ideal midweek dinner that feels restaurant-worthy without the fuss.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 250g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200ml double cream
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • 100ml chicken stock
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside
